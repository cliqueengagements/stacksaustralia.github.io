About Stacks
Stacks is a layer 2 blockchain that enables Smart contracts for Bitcoin bringing functionality to your Bitcoin - Stacks.co

The stacks Australia chapter is the official Stacks Blockchain Community-Led group working to expand the Stacks Community to bring utility beyond P2P payments to Bitcoin. Join the Builders on Bitcoin Movement and be the Revolution

Mentors

The Stacks Australia Mentors program is an initiative that provides guidance and support to aspiring developers and tech enthusiasts in Australia. This program uses Discord as a platform for communication and collaboration between mentors and mentees.
